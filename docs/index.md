# Argus

A non-custodial crypto wallet browser extension with an AI assistant: it
explains every transaction in plain language, warns you before you sign, and
analyzes your activity.

Your recovery phrase and private keys never leave your device — they are
encrypted locally with Argon2id and AES-256-GCM, and the backend has no
endpoint capable of receiving them.
